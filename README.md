## Featured Project: [Chaggerheart](projects/chaggerheart/)

Tabletop RPGs lose players to logistics, not bad stories. Chaggerheart is a web app that automates the bookkeeping so players stay in character and game masters stay in the narrative. Characters resolve abilities on tap, dice rolls apply their effects automatically, and a 5-agent AI pipeline turns session audio into structured notes in about 8 seconds.

Built and shipped solo. 65% of signups complete a character in their first session, and 95% of post-launch organic acquisition arrived after the AI-rebrand. 357 story points across 9 sprints at 22 SP/week sustainable velocity through launch, continuing at 500+ story points across 13+ sprints, 1,990 automated tests across 44 suites.

# About the Founder

**Santiago Vivas-Gonzalez** is a product manager and technical founder based in Chicago. He currently works at Capital One on AI/ML platform strategy while building Chag Innovations LLC on the side.

## Background

Santiago has 5+ years of product experience spanning fintech (Capital One), MedTech (Stryker), SaaS (Golden Software), and startups (Bumpin). At Capital One, he drove 80% adoption of AI tools across 16,000+ associates within one month. At Stryker, he ran 50+ surgeon usability tests on the Mako Surgical Robot and delivered $4M+ in validated product impact. At Golden Software, he shipped a Templater MVP that grew monthly active users by 107%.

He holds a Master of Product Design & Development Management from Northwestern and a BS in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Maryland. He is IBM AI Product Manager certified, a Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO), and Nielsen Norman Group UX certified (NN/g).

## Why Chag Innovations

Santiago founded Chag Innovations because he wanted to build, not just spec. Most product managers hand off requirements and wait. Santiago writes production React and Python, designs the UX, architects the AI pipelines, runs his own sprints, deploys to production, and responds to user feedback, all as a solo founder.

Chaggerheart is the result: a live product with 82 users, 500+ story points of delivered work, 350+ automated tests, and five AI integrations running across four models. It exists because Santiago saw a community (tabletop RPG players) being underserved by existing tools and built the product himself rather than waiting for someone else to fund, staff, and ship it.

The studio's thesis is straightforward: small, underserved communities deserve well-built software, and a product manager who can also engineer is the fastest path to giving it to them.

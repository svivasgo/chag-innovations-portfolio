# About the Founder

**Santiago Vivas-Gonzalez** is a product manager and technical founder based in Chicago. He currently works at Capital One on AI/ML platform strategy while building Chag Innovations LLC on the side.

## Background

Santiago has 5+ years of product experience spanning fintech (Capital One), MedTech (Stryker), SaaS (Golden Software), and startups (Bumpin). He holds a Master of Product Design & Development Management from Northwestern and a BS in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Maryland. He is IBM AI PM certified, a Certified Scrum Product Owner, and Nielsen Norman UX certified.

## Why Chag Innovations

Santiago founded Chag Innovations to build the products he wanted to use. He writes production code, designs UX, runs sprints, and ships to real users. The studio focuses on AI-powered tools for communities that are underserved by existing software.
